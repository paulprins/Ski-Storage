Paul Prins (Freeski520@aol.com),
SkiStorage
Minnesota - Hyland Hills

Then the guys starts telling me about how he just got his first pair of skis with a sidecut on them and he is going over every ting about his skiing. He tells me that he is taking chunks out of his skis and he can't figure out why. So I ask him how he stands and he shows me that he stands with he feet right next to each other. I said that whats probably happening is that his outside skis is over turning and scrapig his inner ski. But then he goes on to reinform me that there are huge chuncks out of his NEW $600 skis. He wants to know if it will ruin his skis. I said that I didn't think so. Then he left.
      I was happy that he was finaly gone and started to talk to my friends again. We were already ripping on this guy but then he came back. He had his NEW $600 skis with him this time. He goes on to show me that his skis had huge chucks taken out of them. By now I'm thinking that he has to be the most retarded person in the world. He pulls his skis apart and then shows me the horible scraches on his skis. I was trying hard not to laugh. The guy had like 4-5 little scraches on his skis. In one spot it had scraped the topsheet particly off. I told the guy that his skis would be fine Then he asked what to do about the core showing so i told ihm not to worie (It wasn't even core. Just the white layer under the topsheat). He said, "Thanks, Now if I only knew how to use them." People like this have always pissed me off. The go and buy the kick ass $900 gear and can't even ski, but I guess I've changed because I was laughing at this guy.
