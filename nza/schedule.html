The 2000 NZ IFSA Season Events

The 2000 Rip Curl World Heli Challenge: A three day heli accessed event that takes place in the alps surrounding Wanaka. The events that make up the heli challenge are; A slope style event, An extreme comp, and a Chinese Downhill. For more info click here or email wanaka.reservations@xtra.co.nz

Rip Curl World Jib Series
Treble Cone Slopestyle: August 6th: The Worlds best skiers and snow boarders ride together along side of amatuers in an awesome dual discipline comp. The terrrain park of TC has been revamped to fit the criteria of the New Zealand International FreeSkiers Association (IFSA). Loaded with hits, rail slides, table tops, and 1/4 pipes the park allows competitors to show all of their talents.
Cardrona 1/2 Pipe: August 10th: More info will be up soon, but no worries it'll be good!
The Wanaka Streetstyle Big Air:  Night of August 12th: Is an event is held downtown of Wanaka. This event goes off! A 70 foot high inramp is constructed, launching skiers over a 40 foot gap. After the landing riders strait run it to a ten foot high 1/4 pipe, skying above a stage! This event is a must with over six thousand spectators and several of the Southern Hemispheres most prolific bands.
