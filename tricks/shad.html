How to Shad Grab/Mute Kang

Ever see Shannon Schrab Air it out of the Quarter pipe with a sweet grab. It's A Shad grab. Totally sweet trick. Not an excuse not to know it

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Bring grabbing leg up.
3)Move you grab hand down and other hand up
4)Grab your ski (Grab)-Hand to Opposite ski in front of leg to outside of toe piece.
5)Release grab and extend leg.
6)Land

Tweaking:
When you go for the grab you do kind of a mute grab thing and pull it over. Make sure that your other leg is pushed all the way out. Cool Pipe move.
