How to English Muffin

Kind of close to an old school trick but a little different. They aren't that hard and look kind of dumb (with a spin it's ok)

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Do a Daffy (One ski forward and one ski back.
3)Put on hand forward to grab the tip and the other back to grab the tail
4)Grab your ski (Grab)-Grab on the very end of the skis if you can
5)Release grab and level legs.
6)Land

Tweaking:
Can't think of any ways to tweak it out.
