How to Ski Switch

Switch is when you ski backwards. This takes a little getting used to but after that it really isn't that hard.

The Steps Switch:
1) Slide around backwards.
2)Try not to lean forward (uphill) it'll only screw you up when you jump.
3)Right before you hit the jump look back up hill. Then when you land try to concentrate on leaning back. yea back. It will help keep you more upright on your skis.

Switch Grab:
Same as a grab but you just have to concentrate a little more. Bring your feet up, grab, and put them down.

Trouble Shooting:
Just keep skiing switch on every thing. If you loose you speed doing the 180 try it different ways like hop turning, Sliding it around, Jumping a 180 to switch. Find which one works best.
