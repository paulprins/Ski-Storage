How to Tip Grab

Tip grabs are fairly simple. You'll have a better chance of getting one if you strech and aren't stiff. Don't look all that bad either.

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Bring Tips up and lean forward.
3)put your hands out to grab tips.
4)Grab your ski (Grab)-Just grab what you can.helps to turn upper body then grab
5)Release grab and level legs.
6)Land
