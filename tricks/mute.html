How to Mute Grab

Every body can mute grab so learn the trick. It takes a while to get down but once you do it is super easy.

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Bring both legs up Try to get you knees close to chest.
3)Move you grab hand down and other hand up
4)Grab your ski (Grab)-Hand to Opposite ski in front of toe piece and pull over.
5)Release grab, straiten skis, and extend legs.
6)Land (Pic)

Tweaking:
We have all seen tweaked mute grabs. They look so nice. Now you want one in you Bag O' Tricks. All you need to do is right when you grab you ski and start to pull it over. You pull it over and back or over and up. I prefer the side(over and up). but the back looks cooler I think.
