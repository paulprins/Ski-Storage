How to Tail Grab

Tail Grabs are pretty lame unless you tweak them out. Remember that cover of freeze with JP and that X Tail Grab. Yea it's sweet.

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Bring tails back like a Back Scratcher
3)Put your hand/s back behind you.
4)Grab your ski (Grab)-Grab on the very end of the ski. Over the tail
5)Release grab and level legs.
6)Land

Tweaking:
2 ways to Tweak it.
#1. Bring one ski back and grab the tail and pull all the way to you back. Then take the other leg and kick it out.

#2. This is the Best one. One of my favorite Tricks. There are 2 ways to do it. You can take both tails back, Right hand to right tail then push it out. OR you can do a Iron Back scratcher and then grab the tail. I think the first looks better.
