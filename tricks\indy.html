How to Indy Grab

The indy grab really is a basic kind of trick. Very easy. Probably one of the first grabs I ever did. Ok now how to do it.

The Steps:
1) Hit the jump.
2)Bring both legs up.
3)Move you grab hand down and other hand up
4)Grab your ski (how to Grab) Same hand to same ski next to boot.
5)Release grab and extend legs.
6)Land

Tweaking:
Instead of bringing up both feet you only bring up the one your going to grab. Then all you do is grab and kick the other leg out.
