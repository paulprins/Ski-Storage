What's in What
Gallery- 4 Galleries. An Amature Gallery where you can post pics of you and friends,  A Gallery Full of Photo Secuences that I make just for the site, My Gallery is where I show my stuff off, and Nybora Gallery with Pics of the Nybora Freestyle Team.

Midwest Competition- Information with Write ups on competitions around the midwest. This place wasn't to hot in 99-00 season but in the 00-01 season it'll go off. There will be a midwest comp schedule posted here and hopefully writeups for every comp on that list. Write one up if you went to one here.

MidwestAlliance- This is the place for all midwest skiers to unite. The idea behind this place is to have one place where skiers from the midwest can come and hang out on the net. There is a forum, Chat room, Comps (same as above), Skier bios so that you can marvel at all of the midwest skiers and see you on the web, and next year we'll have hill reviews and such.
Midwest Skiers check it out.

TerrainPark- This Part is Huge. 5 sections inside and growing. They are a Trick list with definitions , Pictures, and instruction (I'm gradually adding more Trick instruction and Pics), The The Jump Guide with explanations on how to hit/Build/Improve All different types of jumps, Tip Section with skier submitted tips for better freeriding, a skishop that teaches you how to wax and sharpen your skis, and last there is the DownLoads with backgrounds, Calenders, and another cool stuff I can think of to put here. Any Suggestions Email me.

NZA(New Zealand Action)- This section is all about the new school movement to the south of the equator. Basically in New mexico. I got lots of crazy stuff lined up here. John and Josh Mezger (guys who run New Zealand Freeskier Camp) are basically running this section. They are going to do some write ups on comps that happen there. Get interviews and Quotes from the people. Maybe even get some video from this year to me to have put up. I'll be good.

Articles- Read what other people say about skiing stuff (Gear, Stories, Videos) and write your own. I'm going to try to get some interviews with people (Video Producers, Skiers...) and put them here too.

Links- Links to other Kick ass ski sites on the Internet. Sign up for the SkiSites Email list and the banner for my page SkiStorage.
