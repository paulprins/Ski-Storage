Grabs
(Learn how to grab here)

Indy Grab: Same hand to same ski. Next to boot.

Japan: Hand to opposite ski behind leg and next to boot.

Stale Japan: Same as japan but grab in front of the boot. Submitted-Jared Kozel

Jap Slap: Twister (turn skis 90 degrees to the side), then while to the side, do a japan air.

Judo: Grab Opposite ski in front of leg and next to boot.

Shad Grab or Mute Kang: Like a judo, but instead of grabbing under left ski, mute grab it.

Mute Grab: Hand to opposite ski in front of leg and the pulled over to make X

Tia Pan: Like a mute grab but you grab behind between your legs. Still make X.

Cornelius: Make X then a tip grab

Huntony: Mute grab, and kick your right leg back so left ski rests on leg.

Rocket Air (Zute Grab, Zudnick). Mute grab towards the tips of your skis.

Iron Cross (X-Cross, Iron X, Crossed up, X, Iron): Cross your skis making an X.

Tip Grab: Grab your tips.

Tail Grab: Grab tails of skis.

English Muffin: Daffy (one Leg out front one leg out back), then grab tip and tail.

Genie Grab: Screaming seeman, but while legs are crossed, bring one leg up and grab the ski.

Genie Cross: Screaming Seeman with a tweaked out tail grab.

Gypsy Grab: Like a Genie Grab, but bring both legs up and grab.

Crazy Stuff

Screaming Seeman: Do a daffy then cross your skis, (your legs are crossed, left ski is where right should be). Then do another daffy to get out of the cross. You need some mad air to do this.

Switch(Switch Stance): Where you take off backwards, or land backwards.

Fakie: Any thing done the wrong direction. Backside 360, Ski Backwards.

Tweaked out: Any grab, (mute preferably) that is pulled back or to the side.

Spins

180: Spin a half circle, land fakie

360(Helicopter): Spin 1 full circle

540: 1 and a half rotations, land fakie.

720: 2 full circles

900: 2 and a half rotations, land fakie.

1080: 3 full rotations

1260: 3 and a half rotations, land fakie.

1440: 4 full rotations.

The Moseley(3 Mute): It is a 360 with mute grab.

360 Rewind: Do a 180 then do a twister to kind of stall it out. Then finish rotation in same direction.

360 Reverse: Do a 180 the some how you have to get it to go back the other Direction to finish the 3.

Flips and Bio Stuff

BioSpin: Off axis Spin.

Flat Spin (D-Spin): Spinning parallel to the ground. Submitted-Jared Kozel

Front Flip: Flip forwards.

Lawn Dart: It's a super laid out Front flip

Back Flip: Flip Backwards.

Back Flip Froggy: Back flip with a tall cross at the inversion point.

Dude Flip(Picture): Front Flip with a cornileous grab.

Misty Flip: A barel roll under your arm (front) with spin.

Rodeo(Dinner Roll): A barel roll over your shoulder (back) with spin

Lincoln Loop(Sunrise): A sideways spin or barel roll.

Egg Roll: 180 into a lincol loop into a 180. You can add 180 or take away 180 too.
Submited - Craig Weiler

Astroglide: Lincoln Loop 360.

Pipe Move's
Note you can through all of the other tricks in the pipe but these are almost pip only move's.
(How to ski the Pipe)

McTwist: A misty 540.

Alley Oop: A backside (rotate uphill) 180 in a pipe

Flair: Back flip 180.

Chopper: Do a back scratcher(tails touch your back) then grab you tails. Do with no rotation. Float as far as you can and try to go horizontal.
